About Us

In 2020, the BROOK LIVIN team was formed to provide an accessible solution for household safety and protection. WIRECARE was developed in TAIWAN with an intuitive design and highly efficient software, drawing on ZEROPLUS' over 26 years of experience in embedded systems.

"Enjoy Our Tech" "Create a Smart Life!" BROOK LIVIN aims to identify people's needs and develop smart and safe technology. With over 26 years of experience in creating and developing skills, we design smart products to enhance people's lives. Our chameleon logo, like a versatile face, adapts to modern life.

BROOK LIVIN is a lifestyle brand crafted by Zeroplus technology, stemming from 26 years of research, creativity, and passion. We focus on the small details in life, aiming to enhance convenience and create a secure and comfortable tech-infused living experience for you.

SYNERTRON TECHNOLOGY Inc. is the distributor of BROOK LIVIN products in North America and Latin America for reliable quality and service.
